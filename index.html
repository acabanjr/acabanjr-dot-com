As a kid, I had a book full of “My Inventions.” It was full of detailed mock-ups of anything from clothing designs, to video game ideas, to blueprints for skyscrapers, all separated into categories. I’ve always been creative and even more so, organized. Those adjectives describe me best, and I apply them to even the smallest of things.

FASHION DESIGN

With over 15 years of experience in the fashion industry, my journey began with the decision to embrace design over technical roles, despite initial concerns about compensation. This choice has enriched my creative spirit and allowed me to channel my passion into various avenues.

Throughout my career, I've contributed to diverse projects, crafting clothing and accessories for boys, men, and young men. Beyond fashion design, I've left my mark on office spaces, refining design processes, and harnessing skills in graphic, print, and fabric design, as well as technical aspects.
